Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

DailyFit ("we", "us") helps you track meals, nutrition and workouts. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. Questions: hritikukreja18@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address (and, if you use Sign in with Apple or Google, the identifier those services return). Sign-in is required to sync your data.
  • Profile you provide: name, optional username/bio, age, height, weight, goals and preferences.
  • Activity you log: meals and food items, photos of food you scan, workouts and exercises, body-weight entries and progress photos.
  • Apple Health (optional, iPhone only): if you connect Apple Health, we read activity (active energy, steps, exercise minutes), heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, respiratory rate, sleep and body weight to show your daily summary and estimate Strain/Recovery/Body Battery. We only read this data while the app is open, we do not write to Apple Health, and we never use health data for advertising.

How we use it

  • To provide the app: calculate calories/macros and targets, show progress, and sync your data across your devices.
  • Food photo analysis: when you scan or describe a meal, the image or text is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic, the Claude API) to estimate the food and its nutrition. Photos are processed to generate the result and are not used to train third-party models.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in our backend (Supabase — Postgres database and file storage) under your account, protected by row-level security so only you can access it. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the processors that run the app: Supabase (hosting/database/storage) and Anthropic (food analysis). We may disclose information if required by law.

Your choices and rights

  • Export: Profile → Privacy & security → Export my data.
  • Delete your account: Profile → Delete account permanently removes your account and all associated data. This cannot be undone.
  • Disconnect Apple Health: Profile → Apple Health, or revoke access in the iOS Health app at any time.

Data retention

We keep your data until you delete it or delete your account, after which it is removed from our database and file storage.

Children

DailyFit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
